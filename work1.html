About the project

Worldbank is a next-generation decentralized finance (DeFi) platform designed to bridge the gap between complex blockchain protocols and the modern, mobile-first user. By integrating a voice-driven AI core, we transformed the typically friction-heavy banking experience into a seamless, conversational interface that supports 30+ languages.

Driving Growth Through a User-Centric Process

To navigate the growing complexity of modern finance, I utilized a rigorous three-phase methodology designed to unify disparate banking and cryptocurrency experiences into a single, cohesive interface.

Decoding User Intent & Friction

To move beyond assumptions, I conducted a deep dive into how diverse users perceive the intersection of traditional banking and decentralized finance. My research focused on identifying the "trust gap" and the specific points where current menu-driven navigation fails the user.



Defining the Architecture

To solve the complexity found during discovery, I replaced menu-heavy navigation with Natural Language Interaction. By flattening the architecture, the product bridges the gap between traditional banking and decentralized finance efficiency. This minimizes cognitive load, allowing users to manage complex financial tasks with speed and precision.


Visual Identity & Branding

To communicate trust in a decentralized landscape, I developed a visual language that balances security with innovation. The branding focuses on a high-end, minimalist aesthetic that reflects the precision of the underlying AI technology.

High-Fidelity Interface Design

To finalize the vision, I translated the validated wireframes into a high-fidelity interface that embodies Premium Trust and Operational Speed. The UI utilizes a dark, sophisticated palette to emphasize security, while high-contrast accents guide the user toward primary "Voice-to-Action" triggers.

Intent-Driven AI Architecture

To redefine asset management, I engineered an ambient Voice-to-Action layer that entirely replaces deep application hierarchies. By processing natural language commands natively, the platform transforms complex multi-step DeFi transactions and cross-border transfers into a single conversation.
